Cricket has a rich and extensive history that spans centuries. It originated in England
and has evolved into one of the most popular sports globally The history of cricket includes the development of various formats like:-Test matches One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) matche

• Origins:

Cricket can be traced back to 16th-century England.
The first recorded match took place in the 18th century.

• Spread of Cricket:

The sport expanded globally through British colonization. Countries
like :-Australia, India, and the West Indies became cricket-playing nations.

• Limited-Overs Formats:

The 1960s saw the introduction of ODIs, providing a shorter
and more dynamic version of the game. T20 cricket gained popularity in the 21st century.

World Cups:

The Cricket World Cup, inaugurated in 1975, is a major event
in international cricket, bringing together teams from around the world.

• Great Players:

h3>Cricket has produced iconic players like Sir Donald Bradman, Sachin Tendulkar, Sir Vivian Richards, virat kohli
and many more who have left a lasting impact on the game.

• Evolution of Rules:

The laws of cricket have evolved over time,
adapting to changes in the game's format and dynamics.

• Controversies:

Cricket history is not without controversies, including match-fixing scandals and disputes between players and cricket boards.
